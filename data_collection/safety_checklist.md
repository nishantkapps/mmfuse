# Operator Safety Checklist

Before session:
- [ ] Clinician on-site and briefed
- [ ] Emergency stop accessible and tested
- [ ] Robot power and joints checked; safety envelope configured
- [ ] Camera and microphone positioned and tested
- [ ] Sensor cables secured
- [ ] Consent form signed

During trial:
- [ ] Monitor participant comfort continuously
- [ ] Stop immediately on any discomfort or abnormal sensor readings
- [ ] Log any assistance provided

After trial:
- [ ] Save logs and meta.json
- [ ] Note any safety incidents
